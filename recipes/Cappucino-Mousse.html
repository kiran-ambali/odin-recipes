Cappuccino Mousse Recipe

Cappuccino mousse is a light and airy dessert that combines the rich flavors of coffee and chocolate. It's perfect for coffee lovers and makes a delightful treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup brewed espresso, cooled
  • ⅓ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp chocolate syrup
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 2 cups heavy cream, whipped to soft peaks

Steps

  1. Combine espresso, sugar, chocolate, salt, and chocolate syrup.
  2. Gently fold in the whipped cream.
  3. Chill for 20 minutes; serve.
